VOTING EARLY IN-PERSON

For the first time in New Jersey voting history, there are NINE full days of early in-person voting from Saturday, October 23 to Sunday, October 31.

Hours are Monday-Saturday, 10 AM - 8 PM and Sunday 10 AM -6 PM.

Voting in-person on Election Day? Find your polling location.

VOTING ON ELECTION DAY

Cast your votes at your polling location from 6 AM to 8 PM on Tuesday, November 2.
