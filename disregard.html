In-Person Early Voting
  • Ability to cast your vote in person, using a voting machine, during a nine day period prior to Election Day
  • You are allowed to cast your ballot at your county's designated in-person early voting location
  • In Person Early Voting Locations will be open Saturday 23rd through Sunday October 31
  • Hours will be:
    • Monday - Saturday: 10:00am - 8:00pm
    • Sunday: 10:00am - 6:00pm
At Your Polling Place on Election Day:
  • Vote in person at your pollin place, from 6:00 am to 8:00pm on Election Day, November 2.
  • Accomandtions will be made for voters with disabilites
  • Various language trasnlators will be available as well
  • Find your local Polling Place here:(Find Out Where Your Local Polling Place is located)
Vote By Mail Ballot
  • Contact your County Clerk for instructions on recieving an Absentee Ballot
  • Absentee Ballot must be postmarked on or before 8:00pm November 2nd and reciecved by your county's Board of Elections on or before November 8th.
  • Vote and Return your ballot by one of the following ways:
    • By Mail
    • Secure Ballot Drop Box
    • Board Of Elections Office (Find Out Where Your Local Board of Elections Office is Located)
